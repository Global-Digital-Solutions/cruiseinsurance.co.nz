Japan Cruise Insurance

Japanese cruises offer New Zealand travellers deep cultural immersion—ancient temples, contemporary Tokyo energy, traditional gardens, Hiroshima's historical significance, and Japanese hospitality. Japan combines modernity and tradition in unique cultural synthesis.

Key Considerations

Ensure earthquake and tsunami coverage as Japan's Pacific coast occasionally experiences seismic activity. Verify temple exploration and cultural site activity coverage. Confirm coverage for hot spring (onsen) experiences, which are integral to Japanese culture. Look for medical evacuation coverage reaching Japanese trauma centres, though Japan generally has excellent healthcare. Ensure food allergy coverage for seafood and sesame sensitivities common in Japanese cuisine.
